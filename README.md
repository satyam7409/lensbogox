# LensBOGO

LensBOGO helps people make the most of Lenskart's **Buy 1 Get 1 Free** offer by connecting nearby users who are also planning to purchase eyewear.

Instead of letting the second pair go to waste, users can find someone nearby, split the purchase, and effectively save around **50%** on their order.

## How It Works

1. **Sign In**

   * Log in securely using your phone number.

2. **Set Your Location**

   * Share your current location to discover users nearby.

3. **Create or Join a Post**

   * **Create a Post** if you're looking for someone to share the offer with.
   * **Join an Existing Post** from users in your area who are also planning a purchase.

4. **Connect**

   * Chat with your match to decide a convenient time and the nearest Lenskart store to meet.

5. **Purchase Together**

   * Visit the store together, use the Buy 1 Get 1 Free offer, split the cost, and enjoy significant savings.

## Features

* 📱 Phone number authentication
* 📍 Location-based post discovery
* 📝 Create and browse BOGO posts
* 🤝 Match with nearby buyers
* 💬 In-app communication for coordination
* 💸 Save money by sharing Lenskart's Buy 1 Get 1 Free offer

## Why LensBOGO?

Finding someone to share a Buy 1 Get 1 Free offer isn't always easy. LensBOGO makes the process simple by connecting nearby users with the same goal, helping both people save money while making the purchase convenient and hassle-free.
